About Me

Web developer, drone pilot, hobby photographer.

Hi all, I am Herman Vulkers. I am a beginner web developer in Utrecht, The Netherlands. Since a couple of years, I've been dabbling in different coding languages as a hobby. During the COVID pandemic, I decided to take a more serious approach and commit to a career switch, from drone operator to web developer.


During September 2021, I enrolled in a Full Stack Web Development course at Bit-Academy in The Netherlands. Some of the languages that I learned to work with more intensively are HTML, CSS, JavaScript and PHP.


Some random facts about me, in no particular order:


  • I worked as a professional drone pilot for more than 10 years.
  • My favorite Playstation 1 games when I was young were Crash Bandicoot, Tomb Raider, Wipeout & Tony Hawk's Pro Skater.
  • I celebrated the 2018 Vietnamese Women's Day in a remote mountain village near China.
  • I lived in Cape Town, South Africa, while I was doing research for my masters degree.
  • My favorite music genres are rock, metal, techno, hiphop and triphop/downtempo.
